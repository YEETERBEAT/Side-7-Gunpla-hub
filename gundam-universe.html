Exploring the Gundam Universe

Welcome to the vast and exciting world of Gundam! This page serves as an introduction to the various Gundam timelines and how they relate to Gunpla.

Gundam Timelines

The Gundam franchise is divided into several timelines, each with its own unique story and mobile suits. The main timelines are:

  • Universal Century (UC): The original and most extensive timeline, including series like Mobile Suit Gundam, Zeta Gundam, and Unicorn Gundam.
  • After Colony (AC): The timeline for Gundam Wing.
  • After War (AW): The setting for Gundam X.
  • Correct Century (CC): The world of Turn A Gundam.
  • Cosmic Era (CE): The timeline for Gundam SEED and SEED Destiny.
  • Anno Domini (AD): The setting for Gundam 00.
  • Advanced Generation (AG): The world of Gundam AGE.
  • Post Disaster (PD): The timeline for Iron-Blooded Orphans.

How Timelines Relate to Gunpla

The Gundam timelines have a significant impact on Gunpla in several ways:

  1. Mobile Suit Designs: Each timeline features unique mobile suit designs, which are reflected in the Gunpla kits. For example, UC Gundams often have a more "realistic" military look, while CE Gundams tend to have a more stylized appearance.
  2. Kit Availability: The popularity of certain timelines can affect the availability and variety of kits. The UC timeline, being the oldest and most extensive, has the widest range of available kits.
  3. Technology Representation: Different timelines feature different in-universe technologies, which are represented in the Gunpla kits. For instance, UC kits might include psycoframe technology, while CE kits might feature phase shift armor.
  4. Customization Opportunities: Understanding the technology and aesthetics of different timelines can inspire unique customization ideas. For example, you might combine elements from different timelines in a kit-bash project.
  5. Collecting Themes: Many Gunpla enthusiasts focus their collections on specific timelines, allowing them to create cohesive displays or dioramas.
  6. Building Complexity: Some timelines, particularly newer ones, might feature more complex mobile suit designs, which can translate to more intricate and challenging Gunpla builds.

Understanding the various Gundam timelines can enhance your Gunpla experience by providing context for the designs you're building and inspiring new ideas for customization and display. Whether you prefer the classic designs of the Universal Century or the modern aesthetics of more recent timelines, there's a wealth of Gunpla options to explore!
