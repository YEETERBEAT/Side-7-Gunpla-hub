Customizing Your Gunpla

Customizing Gunpla allows you to create unique models that reflect your personal style and vision. This page covers various customization techniques and ideas.

Basic Customization Techniques

  • Color schemes and custom paint jobs
  • Panel lining and detailing
  • Custom decals and waterslides
  • Simple modifications with pla-plate

Advanced Customization

  • Scribing new panel lines
  • Adding custom details with putty and styrene
  • LED modifications
  • Custom weapons and accessories

Design Considerations

When customizing your Gunpla, consider the following:

  • Overall theme or concept
  • Color theory and complementary colors
  • Balance between detail and overall aesthetics
  • Practicality and durability of modifications

Remember, customizing is all about expressing your creativity. Don't be afraid to experiment and try new ideas!
