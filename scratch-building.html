Scratch Building Gunpla

Scratch building is the ultimate challenge in Gunpla, where you create parts or entire models from raw materials. This page will introduce you to the world of scratch building.

Getting Started with Scratch Building

  • Essential tools and materials
  • Planning and designing your scratch build
  • Basic techniques for working with plastics
  • Creating molds and casting parts

Advanced Scratch Building Techniques

  • 3D printing for custom parts
  • Working with different materials (resin, metal, etc.)
  • Creating complex shapes and structures
  • Integrating scratch-built parts with existing kits

Tips for Successful Scratch Building

  • Start small with simple modifications
  • Practice patience and precision
  • Learn from the community and share your progress
  • Don't be afraid to make mistakes - they're part of the learning process

Scratch building opens up endless possibilities for creating unique Gunpla. It's a challenging but rewarding aspect of the hobby!
