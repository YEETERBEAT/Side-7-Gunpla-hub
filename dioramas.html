Creating Gunpla Dioramas

Dioramas allow you to create a complete scene or story with your Gunpla. This page covers the basics of diorama creation and tips for bringing your vision to life.

Planning Your Diorama

  • Choosing a theme or story
  • Sketching your layout
  • Selecting the right scale and materials
  • Balancing composition and visual interest

Building Diorama Elements

  • Creating terrain and landscapes
  • Scratch-building structures and props
  • Using commercial diorama accessories
  • Lighting techniques for added realism

Finishing Touches

  • Painting and weathering diorama elements
  • Adding vegetation and water effects
  • Creating atmospheric effects (smoke, dust, etc.)
  • Positioning Gunpla and other elements for maximum impact

Dioramas are a great way to showcase your Gunpla and tell a story. Let your imagination run wild and create a unique world for your models!
