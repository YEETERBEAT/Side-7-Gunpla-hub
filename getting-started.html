Getting Started with Gunpla

Welcome to the world of Gunpla! This guide will help you take your first steps into building your own Gundam models.

What is Gunpla?

Gunpla is short for "Gundam Plastic Model." It refers to the hobby of building and customizing plastic model kits based on the mobile suits from the Gundam anime series.

Choosing Your First Kit

For beginners, we recommend starting with:

  • High Grade (HG) 1/144 scale kits
  • Entry Grade (EG) kits
  • SD (Super Deformed) kits

Basic Tools You'll Need

  • Nippers or side cutters
  • Hobby knife
  • Sandpaper (various grits)
  • Tweezers

For more detailed information on tools, check out our Tools and Supplies page.
